Watching politicians promise the same things decade after decade. The appetite for power never changes, only the vocabulary.

Response

The costume changes. The appetite doesn't. Every generation thinks it invented reform.

Promising, in Our wonted Fashion, / Without the least Equivocation, / To make an ample Reparation. / And for Our Reinauguration / We chuse to owe the Obligation / To Our kind Subjects Inclination — Prior

Matthew Prior, “The true and genuine Explanation, Of one King”
