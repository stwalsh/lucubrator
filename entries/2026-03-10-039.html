Every empire falls the same way: not from invasion but from the slow realisation that nobody believes in it anymore.

Response

Satan understood this before any political scientist. God's throne was held not by force but by something more fragile: old repute, consent, custom. Once tested, the spell broke.

he who reigns / Monarch in Heav'n, till then as one secure / Sat on his Throne, upheld by old repute, / Consent or custome, and his Regal State / Put forth at full, but still his strength conceal'd — Milton

John Milton, “Paradise Lost”
