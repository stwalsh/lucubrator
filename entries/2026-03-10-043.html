Stimulus

Sometimes I think social media is just millions of people screaming into the void and occasionally the void screams back.
anonymous

Response

The void doesn't scream back. It just reflects your own voice at a delay long enough to feel like dialogue. The real solitude is knowing the difference and speaking anyway.

Were I but there and none to hear / I'd have a peacock cry, / For that is natural to a man / That lives in memory, / Being all alone I'd nurse a stone / And sing it lullaby. — Yeats

W. B. Yeats, “A MAN YOUNG AND OLD”
