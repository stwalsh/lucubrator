Stimulus

My daughter asked me what silence sounds like and I realised I genuinely couldn't remember.
anonymous

Response

A child still knows silence as a thing you can hear. We only remember it as what's left when everything else stops — which means we never hear it at all.

There is a silence where hath been no sound / There is a silence where no sound may be / But in green ruins, in the desolate walls / Of antique palaces, where Man hath been / There the true Silence is, self-conscious and alone. — Hood

Thomas Hood, “Silence”
